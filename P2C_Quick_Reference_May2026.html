P2C Creative Strategy  ·  Quick Reference Card  ·  May 2026

One Mission, Many Expressions.

The one word

Movement.

The tagline

Until all know Jesus.

Use it as a seal — end letters, videos, and ministry footers with it.

The one sentence

Power to Change is a Canadian movement of nine ministries, helping people know Jesus and experience His power to change the world.

The ministry lockup — always

[Ministry name], a Power to Change ministry. FamilyLife Canada, a Power to Change ministry.  ·  DRIME, a Power to Change ministry.  ·  P2C-Students, a Power to Change ministry.

The voice test — read aloud before anything goes out

  • Could a person not yet a Christian read this without flinching at the tone?
  • Could a pastor read this without finding it shallow?
  • Could a P2C staff member read this and feel it sounds like us?

The one paragraph — for grant applications, About pages, and donor decks

Power to Change is a Canadian movement of nine ministries with one shared mission: helping people know Jesus and experience His power to change the world. For 50+ years, our staff and volunteers have shared the gospel, made disciples, and equipped Christ-followers to live on mission across campuses, families, sport, the marketplace, the digital world, and beyond. Today, we are working to ignite ten thousand communities on mission across Canada and the world — until all know Jesus.
